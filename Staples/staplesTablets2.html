Tablets

Keep one of the many tablet options in your satchel, briefcase, or purse for easy access to your personal files or the Internet wherever life takes you. Choose a tablet to fit your work or personal computing needs thanks to a wide range of styles, sizes, and brands including Microsoft, Samsung, and Apple.

Portable
Tuck one of these tablets away in your carry-on for long plane rides or store it in the side pocket of your purse or backpack for quick and easy access to movies, music, or e-books. With ultra-thin designs and lightweight construction, these tablets easily store in your briefcase so that you can keep up on your latest work emails or collaborate with your colleagues while you are out of the office.

Touchscreen Interface
Access your files, view your media, or browse the Internet effortlessly with intuitive touch screen tablets. With many tablets featuring multitouch technology, you can use every finger to quickly zoom, pan, or scroll to easily read your favorite blog or focus in on specific details in your latest marketing project. With a stylus, you can even take notes during meetings or in class and store them for easy access later.

Powerful Options
With tablets from some of the leading brands including Microsoft, Samsung, and Apple, you get the power and options you want and need for work or fun. Choose smaller 7-inch screen sizes for easy storage or select a 12-inch screen for easier readability. For gaming, demanding HD videos, and media-heavy work, choose from a wide range of tablets featuring powerful integrated graphics for crystal-clear display of all of your media. You can even take photos on vacation or video chat with distant family thanks to front- and rear-facing camera options.

Probably the best known tablet is the Apple iPad. The iPads software has the same style user interface as both the iPhone and iPod Touch (iOS), along with access to Apples vast App store. iPads are available in both WiFi and 3G versions for maximum portability. Apple has just introduced the iPad Air 2 and iPad mini 3. The iPad Air 2 is thinner and more powerful. Both iPad Air 2 and iPad mini 3 introduce enhanced breakthrough security with Touch ID technology. Use your fingerprint to unlock iPad Air 2 or iPad mini 3. Models come in silver, gold or space gray.

Android tablets are available from a number of well-known brands such as Samsung, Asus, Acer, Google and many more. Like the Apple iPad Android tablets are easy to use but allow for further customization so you can get the most out of it, whilst coming in at a slightly lower price point than their counter part. Android tablets such as the Samsung Galaxy Tab 4 also have access to a wide range of both productivity and fun related APPs through the Google Play App store.

Staples also carries a large selection of tablet accessories such as iPad cases & covers in addition to top accessories for many other models. This includes all types of cases and covers, screen protectors and stylus pens and screen protectors in a variety of styles and colors, all designed to protect your favorite new devices. So whatever brand or model you choose, you are sure to find the right accessories that will protect and preserve your tablet.
